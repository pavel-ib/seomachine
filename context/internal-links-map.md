## Internal Linking Best Practices

### Priority Levels

**Tier 1 – Link Often** (3–5 links per article):
- Relevant product pages (match product to article topic)
- Pillar blog content in the same topic cluster
- Collection pages when discussing product categories

**Tier 2 – Link Sometimes** (1–2 links per article):
- Compare page (when helping readers choose)
- Related blog articles in adjacent clusters
- FAQ page (when answering common objections)

**Tier 3 – Link Rarely**:
- Homepage
- Blog category index pages
- Careers, privacy policy, or account pages

### Linking Guidelines

**Do**:
- Link to the most relevant product page when discussing supplies or kit features
- Use descriptive, keyword-rich anchor text (not "click here")
- Link to blog articles that expand on a topic mentioned in passing
- Distribute links naturally throughout the article
- Link to the compare page when a reader might be deciding between kits

**Don't**:
- Force product links into every paragraph
- Use identical anchor text for the same URL across articles
- Link to the same page more than once in a single article
- Add more than 2 links per paragraph
- Link only to product/sales pages—balance with educational content
