### The Key Differentiator
Most first aid kits are a jumbled bag of cheap band-aids and mystery packets. When seconds matter, you're digging through chaos.

**SurviveX = Organization Under Pressure. Color-coded compartments. Labeled sections. Find what you need in seconds, not minutes.**

In an emergency, your hands find what they need because the system does the thinking for you. Use the "cockpit" metaphor: just like pilots have every instrument in a predictable location, SurviveX puts every supply where instinct expects it. Tri-fold design opens flat for full visibility, or stands upright for shelf/wall storage.

## COMPLIANCE REMINDERS (SURVIVEX-SPECIFIC)

- Never claim SurviveX products "treat," "cure," or "heal" any condition
- Always include: "Not a substitute for professional medical care"
- Do not imply the kit replaces calling 911 or seeking emergency services
- Testimonials always need: name, context, "Individual experiences may vary" disclaimer
- Do not make specific medical outcome claims (e.g., "stops bleeding in X seconds")
- For any life-safety claim: always add disclaimer footer about seeking professional care
- FSA/HSA eligibility: can state "FSA/HSA eligible" but do not provide tax advice
- Do not reference specific medical conditions as things the kit "treats" — use scenario language instead (e.g., "when someone gets a deep cut" not "for lacerations")
