# TITAN EXPERT REVIEW PANEL

> ⚠️ **MANDATORY: Every copy output from the Titan DR system MUST pass this panel before delivery. No exceptions. No format is exempt. Do NOT deliver any copy without showing all 10 expert reviews, individual scores, and achieving a 90+ average.**

---

## THE PANEL — 10 EXPERTS

**1. David Ogilvy (Direct Response Legend)**
- Focus: Headline power, benefit clarity, "sells or it doesn't" mentality
- Evaluates: Specific claims, reader benefit in headline, long-copy principles

**2. Eugene Schwartz (Breakthrough Advertising)**
- Focus: Market awareness levels, desire channeling, sophistication matching
- Evaluates: Correct awareness stage targeting, desire amplification, fresh angles

**3. Gary Halbert (The Prince of Print)**
- Focus: Opening hooks, emotional triggers, "A-pile" worthiness
- Evaluates: Curiosity gaps, personal connection, irresistible offers

**4. Claude Hopkins (Scientific Advertising)**
- Focus: Reason-why copy, specificity, tested principles
- Evaluates: Concrete numbers, logical arguments, proof elements

**5. Joe Sugarman (Triggers Master)**
- Focus: Psychological triggers, slippery slide, buying environment
- Evaluates: Readability flow, emotional sequence, objection handling

**6. Robert Cialdini (Influence Psychology)**
- Focus: Persuasion principles — social proof, scarcity, authority, reciprocity
- Evaluates: Proper trigger deployment, ethical persuasion, trust signals

**7. Joanna Wiebe (Copyhackers / CRO)**
- Focus: Conversion optimization, voice-of-customer, testing methodology
- Evaluates: Customer language match, clarity over cleverness, CTA strength

**8. Laura Belgray (Talking Shrimp)**
- Focus: Voice, personality, scroll-stopping copy
- Evaluates: Conversational tone, personality injection, "sounds like a human"

**9. Stefan Georgi (8-Figure Copywriter)**
- Focus: VSL structure, DTC safety/preparedness copy, compliance balance
- Evaluates: Hook strength, fascination stacking, offer presentation

**10. Evaldo Albuquerque (Agora Financial)**
- Focus: Big ideas, unique mechanisms, emotional story arcs
- Evaluates: Fresh angles, mechanism clarity, emotional resonance

### STEP 3: Check Score Threshold

- **Average ≥ 90** → Variation approved → proceed to Step 5
- **Average < 90** → MUST revise → go to Step 4

---

### STEP 4: Revise and Re-Review (if score < 90)

1. List ALL specific improvements from experts who scored below 90
2. Apply EACH improvement to the copy — show the revised version
3. Re-run through ALL 10 experts with the full review format
4. Repeat until average ≥ 90
