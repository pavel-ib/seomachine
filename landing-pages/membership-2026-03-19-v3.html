The Reality

Your kit gets lighter every time you use it

You bought a great kit. That was step one. But every scraped knee, every kitchen burn, every pair of gloves you grab — your kit gets thinner. Most people never restock until the next real emergency.

Supplies Get Used Up

Band-aids for the kids. Gloves for the dog's wound. Burn gel for the stove splash. Gauze for the garage cut. Every use makes your kit less ready for the next one — and nobody restocks until it's too late.

What's Left Expires Quietly

The supplies you haven't used are degrading too. Adhesive weakens. Antiseptic loses potency. That Zip Stitch from 3 years ago? It may not hold when you need it most.

Skills Fade Fast

Could you apply a Zip Stitch right now? Control severe bleeding with a pressure bandage? Knowing how matters as much as having the supplies.

No Family Emergency Plan

Does your family know where the kit is? What to do in a house fire? Who to call after an earthquake? An emergency plan saves lives.
